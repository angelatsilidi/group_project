Terraforma Festival 5-7 July

Terraforma Website

Friday night acts and all day music over the weekend starting at 10am and ending late. Terraforma showcases a diverse group of electronic musicians and experiential acts such as Stargate and Laurie Anderson. Over the course of the weekend there are also lectures taking place led by musicologists and scientists shedding light on topics from vocal experimentation in music to the complexities of plant communities and individuals.
