JANUARY

PHILLIPINES

Binalabal Festival





Date: January 1st 2020
Venue: Tuleda, Misamis

It dates back to the 1930s when townsfolk began wearing masks to disguise their identity, slowing evolving over the years to become a general celebration in the hope of prosperity for the coming year. The festival begins the night before with the Masquerade Ball and continues until noon the following day when a Grand Parade goes through the town of Tuledo.

FEBRUARY

FLORIDA, USA

Art & Seafood on the Waterfront




Date: February 22-23, 2020
Venue: Safety Harbor, Florida

This FREE 2-day festival includes a juried art show, a Talent show, Music stage and Seafood festival featuring some of the best local restaurants in Tampa Bay. Local, national and international artists will be exhibiting and selling their work and competing for 1st 2nd and 3rd place cash prizes. We have fresh seafood items from Bonefish Grill, Daydreamers Café & Grill, and many others including some delicious food trucks like Got Lobstah. There are also interactive kids’ arts activities and a musical stage on Saturday night from 7-9 pm and Sunday from 5-6pm, Safety Harbor’s Talent show Saturday highlighting the top 15 acts from the Tampa Bay area. Waterfront Park directly behind Safety Harbor Resort & Spa.

July

Milan, Italy

Terraforma Festival

Date: July 5, 6, 7th 2020
Venue: Villa Arconati

Friday night acts and all day music over the weekend starting at 10am and ending late. Terraforma showcases a diverse group of electronic musicians and experiential acts such as Stargate and Laurie Anderson. Over the course of the weekend there are also lectures taking place led by musicologists and scientists shedding light on topics from vocal experimentation in music to the complexities of plant communities and individuals. The festival aims to increase the public’s awareness on sustainable living by providing workshops, recycling systems and solar panelled camping facilities. Villa Arconati is a 1km walk from the closest train station, Bollate Nord, or a shuttle bus can be taken from here.

August

Goiania, Brazil

Bananada Festival

Date: Date To Be A nnounced
Venue: Passeio das Aguas Shopping

Festival Bananada is a week-long music festival held in Goiânia, Brazil. For nearly two decades, the festival has encouraged and supported independent music, showcasing local talent and bringing national and international artists to the city year on year. Across the course of the week, the festival transforms Goiânia into a hub of music, art, cuisine and culture, as well as playing host to skateboarding championships and some of the country's best tattoo artists.

September

Malawi, Africa

Lake of Stars

Date: 27, 28, 29th September
Venue: Kachere Kastle

The Lake of Stars Project was set up in 2003 to attract more international visitors to Malawi. Lake of Stars provides local artists with a unique platform to showcase their art to a global audience, as well as attracting high profile names to perform from across the world, often for the first time in Africa. Previous international performers include Andy Cato of Groove Armada, Beverley Knight, Bombay Bicycle Club, Christian Tiger School, Felix B of Basement Jaxx and Major Lazer Soundsytem.

October

Australia

Yours & Owls Festival

Date: 3rd - 5th October
Venue: Stuart Park, Wollongong

Yours and Owls is Wollongong's biggest and most exciting annual music festival, which runs for two days across the October long weekend. It features art installations, food and markets stalls and attracts Australia's biggest bands and International touring musicians.

November

Iceland

Iceland Airwaves

Date: 4th - 7th November
Venue: Reykjavík, Iceland

Iceland Airwaves Festival is the world’s most northerly music showcase and industry festival, situated halfway between North America and Europe. Iceland Airwaves brings together the country’s brightest emerging musical talent and forward-thinking international acts. Each November for four days and nights, downtown Reykjavík comes alive, filled non-stop with music, with performances hosted everywhere from tiny record stores and art museums, to cool bars and stately churches, to nightclubs and large scale venues.

December

Canada

Winter Festival of Lights

Date: Nov 16th - January 12th
Venue: Niagara Parkway, Niagara Falls

The most enchanting of Niagara attractions, the Ontario Power Generation Winter Festival of Lights, has become a Holiday tradition for over one million visitors from around the globe. Experience the spectacular light displays, Light Art, interactive illuminations, the Fallsview Sound and Light Show, festive events and over 3 million lights displayed along the Niagara Parkway, Dufferin Islands and Niagara Falls.
